# How Your WhatsApp Agent Works — For Business Owners

## In Plain Language

Your WhatsApp number acts like a smart receptionist that never sleeps. When a customer sends a message, the system:

1. **Understands** whether they need *tours & experiences* or *car rental*
2. **Responds** in a warm, premium way—in the same language they wrote
3. **Flags** complex or high-value enquiries so your staff can take over personally
4. **Logs** everything so you can see all conversations and follow up

---

## Two Businesses, One Inbox

| Tourism | Car Rental |
|---------|------------|
| Holiday packages, visas, desert safaris, groups | Vehicles, pricing, chauffeurs, bookings, cancellations |

Messages are routed into one of these two verticals automatically. If the AI can’t tell (e.g. very vague messages), it asks one simple clarifying question instead of guessing.

---

## When Your Team Gets Involved

The AI escalates to a human when it detects:

- **Group 10+ people** — high-value, needs personal handling  
- **Rental 7+ days** — long-term, custom terms  
- **Cancellation disputes** — sensitive, needs empathy  
- **VIP or corporate tone** — deserves priority  
- **Unclear intent** — asks a quick clarification instead of guessing  

Staff see a short brief explaining the situation so they can pick up smoothly.

---

## Off-Topic or Difficult Messages

- **Vague / mixed** → One warm clarifying question, no robotic answers  
- **Wrong language** → Always replies in the customer’s language  
- **Off-topic** (e.g. weather, politics) → Polite acknowledgment, brief mention of services  

---

## What You Can Do

- **Dashboard** — Review all enquiries, filter by Tourism / Car Rental / Escalated, and see why each escalation happened  
- **Knowledge base** — Update company info (packages, fleet, policies); the AI uses this to give accurate answers  
- **Logs** — All conversations are saved for follow-up and reporting  

---

*Your AI assistant aims to feel premium and human—never robotic. It’s there to qualify leads, answer common questions, and hand off smoothly when a person is needed.*
